About RK Brothers

Koramangala's trusted self-drive service

Our Story

RK Brothers Cars Rental is an owner-managed service born from a simple belief: renting a car should be as easy as messaging a friend. We started in 2020 with just 3 cars and a commitment to transparency. Today, we serve hundreds of happy customers across Koramangala with a fleet of clean, new-model vehicles — all without the hassle of deposits or hidden charges.

2020: Started with 3 cars
2022: 200+ trips completed
2024: 500+ happy customers
2025: 8+ car fleet

Our Mission

To provide safe, affordable, and hassle-free self-drive rentals with full transparency. We believe everyone deserves the freedom to drive without worrying about excessive paperwork, surprise fees, or unreliable vehicles. That's why we personally ensure every car is sanitized, serviced, and ready for your journey.
