Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

Do I need to pay a deposit?
We require a refundable ₹6,000 deposit OR you can submit your Bike RC as security. The deposit is fully refunded when you return the car in good condition.
What documents do I need?
You need a valid driving license (original) and any government-issued ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport). We'll verify them before handover.
How many kilometers are included?
All our rentals include 400 km per day as standard. Extra kilometers are charged as per our clear policy, which we share with you on WhatsApp before you confirm your booking.
Is doorstep pickup available?
Yes, we offer free doorstep pickup and drop within Koramangala. Just mention your location when booking.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 6 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 6 hours are subject to a 20% charge. Full details in our policy page.
