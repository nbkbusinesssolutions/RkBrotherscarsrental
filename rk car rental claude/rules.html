Rules & Regulations

Please read and understand all rules before booking. By renting a vehicle from RK Brothers, you agree to these terms.

Booking & Payment

1

Advance Payment Required

You will have to pay ₹6,000 advance amount to book a vehicle.

2

Non-Refundable Advance

Advance amount is NOT refundable after payment. Please be sure before booking.

3

Full Payment at Pickup

The remaining full amount will have to be cleared when the vehicle is picked up.

Vehicle Pickup Requirements

4

Documents Required at Pickup

At the time of vehicle pickup, you must bring:

  • Original Driving Licence
  • PAN Card
  • Two Wheeler Vehicle (for deposit)

Damage Policy

5

Vehicle Damage Charges

If there are any scratches, dents, or damage to the vehicle, you will have to pay for the repairs immediately.

Speed Limit

6

Maximum Speed: 100 km/h

The speed limit for all rental vehicles is 100 km/h.

If you exceed 100 km/h, there will be a charge of ₹1,000 for each speed violation.

Terms & Conditions

No Drink & Drive

Strictly prohibited. Do not consume alcohol and drive the car.

Compulsory Seat Belts

Seat belts must be worn by all passengers at all times.

No Rash Driving

Drive safely and responsibly. Rash or negligent driving is strictly prohibited and may result in penalties.

Quick Summary

  • ₹6,000 advance to book (non-refundable)
  • Full payment at vehicle pickup
  • Bring: Original DL, PAN Card, Two Wheeler for deposit
  • Speed limit: 100 km/h (₹1,000 fine per violation)
  • Damage charges payable immediately
  • No drink & drive, no rash driving
  • Seat belts compulsory for all
